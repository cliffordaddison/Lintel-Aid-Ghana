Who We Are

Lintel Aid Ghana started from a grassroots passion to support children and youth. Inspired by Linda Teye's journey of supporting underprivileged communities, we have grown into a structured NGO focused on education, economic empowerment, and poverty alleviation across Ghana.

Founder & Team

Meet the people behind Lintel Aid Ghana – our founder, leadership, and core team.

Founder

Linda Teye

Linda Teye is the founder of Lintel Aid Ghana. Her humanitarian leadership and dedication to supporting children and youth have been recognized through multiple awards, including the Coca-Cola Achievers Award (2017), Top 30 Under 30 – The Future of Ghana (2018), Ghana Humanitarian and Business Award (2018), Winner of the Community Development Category – 40Under40 Awards (2018), Youth Leadership Parliament – Woman of the Year (2018), Youth Empowerment Humanitarian Award (2021), and Humanitarian Awards Global – Certificate of Achievement (2021).

Under her vision, Lintel Aid Ghana has grown from grassroots beginnings into a credible, youth-driven NGO transforming lives through education, women empowerment, and community support across Ghana.

Vision & Mission

Our long-term goals are tied to the UN Sustainable Development Goals and scaling impact through partnerships.

visibility

Vision

An impactful world through partnership and collaboration. We aim to scale our programmes and deepen partnerships with schools, corporates, and government to reach more underprivileged children, women, and communities.

track_changes

Mission

Creating a positive impact and nurturing lives through empowering, educating and supporting the less privileged. We focus on Quality Education (SDG 4), Eradicating Poverty (SDG 1), and Decent Work & Economic Growth (SDG 8).
