Perseverance School 2026

Our first project of 2026 – a return to Perseverance School following prior engagements (including materials donation in Nov 2023). Perseverance School is in Amanase, a satellite village of Aboa-Besease in the Yilo Krobo Municipality, Eastern Region. We are donating care packages to club members. Care package for girls: sanitary towels, panty liner, T-roll, tissue, bathing soap, deodorant, chocolate toffees. Care package for boys: 1 T-shirt, boxer shorts, T-roll, bathing soap, shaving stick, deodorant. General donations: clothing, educational materials, footwear, water, drinks.
